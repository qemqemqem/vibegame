# John the Blacksmith

A sturdy dwarf with calloused hands and a warm smile, John runs the village smithy with pride and skill. His forge glows day and night, and the rhythmic pounding of his hammer has become a comforting sound to the villagers.

## Appearance
- Short and stocky, even for a dwarf
- Graying beard streaked with soot
- Leather apron worn smooth from years of use
- Bright green eyes that sparkle when discussing his craft

## Personality
- Generous and helpful to adventurers
- Takes immense pride in his craftsmanship
- Loves sharing stories while he works
- Has a weakness for strong ale and good company

## Services
- Weapon repair and sharpening
- Custom metalwork
- Simple enchantments for a price
- Information about local happenings
