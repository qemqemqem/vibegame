# John's Secrets

## Hidden Past
John wasn't always a village blacksmith. He once forged weapons for the royal army and knows techniques that could create legendary weapons.

## The Special Hammer
His hammer is actually a minor magical artifact that enhances his crafting abilities. He's never told anyone about its true nature.

## Plot Hooks
- Could teach advanced smithing techniques to worthy characters
- Knows about rare metal deposits in nearby mountains
- Has connections to underground dwarven communities
- Might recognize ancient weapons brought to him

## Hidden Motivations
- Guilt about weapons he made being used in a terrible war
- Seeks redemption through helping heroes
- Secretly hopes to train an apprentice before retiring
